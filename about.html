About Our Organization

NewsGrid is a news aggregator that was started in 2019 with one goal in mind. That goal is to provide the latest news in a clear and readable format with any fluff. This is very easily accomplished, but most people have no clue how to do so and just deal with the clutter.

Most news sites today don't just provide the news, but do their best to make money through advertising. This not only annoys readers, but often makes them go to other sources to get the news. We recognized this problem and decided to take action. What if there was a way to read the news without having to install extensions or perform any loopholes just to read the news? Well, now there is and we are proud to present this to you our friends. Take a look around and enjoy your time here. Don't forget to subscribe to our newsletter to get the latest breaking news!
